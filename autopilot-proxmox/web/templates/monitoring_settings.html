Kerberos keytab health

The autopilot container authenticates to AD via a Kerberos keytab at /etc/krb5.keytab, kept fresh by an in-container refresher that runs daily. The refresher uses the AD credential above to read the gMSA's msDS-ManagedPassword and derive new AES keys before the 30-day rotation window.
