Answer floppy cache

Each provisioned sequence compiles to an autounattend.xml delivered to the clone as a FAT12 floppy image at /var/lib/vz/snippets/autopilot-unattend-<sha256>.img on the Proxmox host. Windows Setup lands on the floppy at position 4 of its answer-file search (removable read/write media). Two VMs whose compiled payloads are byte-identical share one floppy.

Entries flip to unused as soon as the provision job finishes — the cleanup step strips the floppy off the VM once Windows has consumed it. Prune unused rows to reclaim the cached .img on disk; they'll be rebuilt on demand the next time a matching sequence compiles.
