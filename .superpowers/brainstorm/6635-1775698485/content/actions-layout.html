Behavior Notes

  • Running VMs show: Shutdown, Force Stop, Reset, Capture Hash, Console, Delete
  • Stopped VMs show: Start, Delete
  • Shutdown = ACPI graceful shutdown   Force Stop = immediate power off
  • Delete and Force Stop get confirmation dialogs
  • All icons have tooltips on hover
